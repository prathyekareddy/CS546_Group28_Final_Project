SubSplit

Welcome to SubSplit! SubSplit, short for subscription split, enables users to come together and split subscriptions of different platforms among themselves, including but not limited to, music applications, entertainment (television or films), OTT platforms, digital content providers, network provider plans, and so much more!

These are just some of the perks of using SubSplit:

  • Find co-subscribers who share similar interests to share the membership
  • Find users to fill available slots in subscription plans
  • Manage payments for different subscriptions easily
  • And access their favourite services at a lower rate!
